T-MATS: Temp2Enth Library Block
This block emperically calculates enthalpy (h) from temperature (T) and fuel to air ratio (FAR). Enthalpy will be total or static based on input temperature (total "Tt" or static "Ts").

Temp2Enth Inputs:
Temp2Enth InputDescription
TTemperature [degR]
FARFuel to Air Ratio[frac]

Temp2Enth Outputs:
Temp2Enth OutputDescription
hEnthalpy [BTU/lbm]
