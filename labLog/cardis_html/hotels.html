IBIS Hotel

The hotel stay is not included in the registration fee. You are encouraged to book a room in the IBIS Zentral hotel, where participants are granted a limited number of rooms at a special rate. This hotel is next to the central station and will also be used as meeting point for some social events and the excursion.

Other Hotels in Bochum

You can find other hotels in Bochum at different categories and prices. Also very close to the central station is the Mercure (a bit pricier). Within a 7 minutes walk to the central station in the local pub area is the art Hotel Tucholsky (also a bit pricier).
