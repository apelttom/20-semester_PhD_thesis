T-MATS: Enth2Temp Library Block
This block emperically calculates temperature (T) from enthalpy (h) and fuel to air ratio (FAR). Temperature will be total or static based on input enthalpy (total "ht" or static "hs").

Enth2Temp Inputs:
Enth2Temp InputDescription
hEnthalpy [BTU/lbm]
FARFuel to Air Ratio[frac]

Enth2Temp Outputs:
Enth2Temp OutputDescription
TTemperature [degR]
