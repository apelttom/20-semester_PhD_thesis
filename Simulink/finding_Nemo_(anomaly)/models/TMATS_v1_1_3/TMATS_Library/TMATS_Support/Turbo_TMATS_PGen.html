T-MATS: Power Generator Library Block
This block converts a power demand with rotational speed into a torque and can be used to simulate a power generator.

PGen Inputs:
PGen InputDescription
PowerDMDPower demand[Watt]
NmechMechanical shaft speed[rpm]

PGen Outputs:
PGen OutputDescription
Req_TorqRequired Torque [lbf*ft]

PGen Mask Variables:
PGen Mask VariableDescription
Eff_MGenerator efficiency [frac]
